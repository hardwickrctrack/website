HRCT track use Rules


Please Note: Failure to comply with these few simple rules may, at the sole discretion of the organisers, result in a request leave site with no refund on fees due


No drugs or alcohol permitted on site


  1. Marshalling is a requirement of track use, when you have been using track, please help out by marshalling for others to use the track.
  2. Fires and BBQs are not permitted.
  3. No dogs, (except service dogs) allowed on site.
  4. All rubbish to be taken away with you. Absolutely no rubbish to be left on site.
  5. Nobody other than marshals may at any time enter the track area, Marshall's must wear Hi-Viz at all times whilst on track.
  6. Under no circumstances may the cars be run on the ground, under their own power outside of the marked track area.
  7. Track may only be used when 2 marshals are present
  8. Track direction must be followed at all times, any horseplay or deliberate dangerous driving will result in removal, off the track.

PLEASE NOTE: By signing, you are accepting, whilst using any of the HRCT site:

You accept and understand that due to the nature of the hobby, some risk of personal injury is possible and sometimes unavoidable and that no claim will be made. or liability alleged against any individual, the organisers or land owner, for injury or damage to property.
